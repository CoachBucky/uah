Course Overview

AP Calculus AB focuses on understanding calculus concepts through a multi-representational approach. Concepts, results, and problems are expressed graphically, numerically, analytically, and verbally.

üìö Topics Covered ‚ñº
  • Limits: Understanding behavior of functions
  • Derivatives: Rates of change and applications
  • Definite Integrals: Area under curves and accumulation
  • Fundamental Theorem of Calculus: Connecting derivatives and integrals

üìù Assessment Details ‚ñº

Tests: Each test contains multiple choice and free response questions with "AP flavor." Some questions may be similar to homework and practice problems, while others require synthesis of previous and new concepts. Tests may include both calculator and non-calculator sections.

Daily Work: AP style bell ringers are used throughout the year for review. Regular homework assignments reinforce concepts and prepare students for assessments.

Final Exam Policy:
A comprehensive final exam (20% of final grade) is required for:
